About Us

Preamble

ISLAMIC SITES CONSERVATION is a Non-Government Organization involved in preservation and maintenance of Islamic Historical sites and Monuments. ISC is also educating people on introduction of Islam in our Country through early evidences of Historical heritage.

The Organization established on 23rd March 2007 and got Registration on 24th October 2008 with the Registration Number 3568 under Trustees Incorporation Act (Cap. 318 R.E 2002).

WABILLAH TAWFIQ
